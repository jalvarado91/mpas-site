Benefits of Studying with Us

All of our teachers are experienced performers in their field of expertise. Students will learn from teachers who bring in the excitement and energy from the real world.

Our classes are kept small for personalized attention. Some dance classes include a live drummer!

Our teaching method is a learning process which is transferable to everyday life. We are not "show" driven. Your performance skills will be developed through various free showcases. There is no lasting educational benefit going from show to show.

The students safety and well being is our number one concern, therefore, we focus on proper technique and use professional equipment including a sprung dance floor.

The curriculum is designed to enhance needed skills for Thespians, Magnet programs, and auditions. Our Pre-Professional program gives students the necessary skills to prepare for college and professional careers.

We will offer workshops given by working professionals in the business from New York to Los Angeles and around the world. Guest artists include performers from The Rockettes, Chicago, Beauty & The Beast, Les Miserables, Millie, A Chorus Line, Ragtime and many more. Along with professional training in Uta Hagen Method, and Commercial Acting Techniques.
